Designing Sociotechnical Systems: Study | Approach A

Before you start the study

Read the consent form, sign and return it to the investigator.

During the study

In this study, you will learn to design a sociotechnical system. The study is divided in three phases. In each of the phase, your task is to go through the approach tutorial, and apply the learning to design a normative specification for a given scenario. As you start designing the specification, put the start time on the sheet given to you. After designing the specification for each phase, put the end time on the sheet given to you, and complete the post-survey based on your experience for that particular phase.
